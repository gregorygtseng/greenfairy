What is a Climate Score?

Climate Counts uses a 0 - 100 point scale and 22 criteria to determine if companies have:

MEASURED their climate "footprint",

REDUCED their impact on global warming,

SUPPORTED progressive climate legislation, and publicly

DISCLOSED their climate actions.
